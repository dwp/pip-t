Thank you for starting your Personal Independence Payment (PIP) claim.

What you need to do

Fill in the How your disability affects you form: [LINK TO DOCUMENT 1: INTERACTIVE PIP2 PDF FORM]

You can fill in the form on a computer, tablet or phone. You do not need to print it out. The form works best on a computer or tablet. More information about completing the form can be found in:

  • the How your disability affects you booklet [LINK TO DOCUMENT 2: INFORMATION BOOKLET]
  • PIP additional information [LINK TO DOCUMENT 3: ADDITIONAL INFORMATION]
  • a flyer that explains the types of supporting information you should send [LINK TO DOCUMENT 4: FLYER]

Get help

You can ask a friend, relative, carer, representative, or local support organisation to help you complete the form either over the phone or in person if they live with you or visit you for a medical reason or to provide care.

Get help with filling in the form and providing supporting information: [LINK TO DOCUMENT 2: INFORMATION BOOKLET]

Information to support your claim

Get help with the types of supporting information you should send: [LINK TO DOCUMENT 4: FLYER]

Do not delay sending your form back if you are waiting for more supporting information.

Only send us supporting information you have already.

Do not contact your GP or health professional to ask for new or additional information as they may charge you for this.

Do not leave the house to collect supporting information or contact your doctor or hospital during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

What happens next

We may call you to check you have received this email and if you need any support.

When you have returned your form, you will get an email or text message to confirm that we have received your form.
