Guidance

The form works best on a computer or tablet. You do not need to print it out. Save the form somewhere you can easily find it later.

You can ask a friend, relative, carer, representative, or local support organisation to help you fill in the form over the phone. They should only help you in person if they live with you or visit you for a medical reason or to provide care.

Watch a DWP video on YouTube about providing information to support your claim

Do not delay sending your form back if you are waiting for more supporting information.

Only send us supporting information you have already.

Do not contact your GP or health professional to ask for new or additional information as they may charge you for this.

Do not leave the house to collect supporting information or contact your doctor or hospital during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.
