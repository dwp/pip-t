Send your PIP documents

Use this service to upload and send your Personal Independence Payment (PIP) documents to the Department for Work and Pensions. These can be:

  • your filled-in ‘How your disability affects you’ (PIP2) form
  • any supporting evidence for your PIP application

Only use the service if you’ve already applied for PIP and you’ve agreed to upload your documents online.

You can use this service more than once to upload and send documents at different times.

Before you start

Take photos or scans of any paper documents that you need to send as supporting evidence.

Make sure you save all the documents you need, including your completed ‘How your disability affects you’ form, to the device you are using.
